# Global Beverage Sales & Profitability Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview
This project delivers a comprehensive **Power BI sales intelligence dashboard** for a global beverage company operating across **North America, Europe, and the Middle East**. The dashboard tracks **revenue vs target, profitability, product performance, retail channel comparison, and sales manager rankings** from **2023–2025**.

This solution replaces fragmented Excel reporting with a **centralized, executive-ready analytics platform**.

---

## Business Objectives
- Track **actual sales vs target performance**
- Monitor **gross & net profitability**
- Identify **top-performing products and countries**
- Compare **Hypermarkets vs Supermarkets**
- Evaluate **sales manager performance**
- Analyze **quarterly and yearly sales trends**

---

## Key KPIs
- Total Sales: **$270.4M**
- Target Sales: **$282.1M**
- Gross Profit: **$131.2M**
- Net Profit: **$101.8M**
- Gross Margin: **48.5%**
- Net Profit Margin: **37.6%**

## Key Business Insights
- USA is the top-performing market.
- Still Water generates the highest revenue.
- Some countries operate through a single retail channel.
- Quarterly sales show continuous growth from 2023 to 2025.
- Top sales managers outperform low performers by over 8x revenue.
